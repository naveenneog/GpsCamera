Everything a GPS camera should do

Precise, readable, shareable — and pleasant to use.

📸

Geotagged capture

A clean map-on-the-left card: locality, full address, Lat / Long, altitude, timestamp and live temperature — burned onto the photo.

🗺️

Live mini-map

A real OpenStreetMap thumbnail of your exact spot, shown live and stamped onto the shot.

👆

Open in Maps

Tap the map to jump straight to the coordinates in Google Maps — the link is embedded in the photo too.

🎥

Photo & video

Switch to video and record clips to your album. Pinch the preview to zoom in and out.

🖼️

Full-screen gallery

Tap any shot to open it, swipe between photos, pinch-zoom, then open in Maps or share.

🔁

Portrait & landscape

Rotates smoothly and reflows the controls to a side rail in landscape.

🧲

Move & resize

Drag the info block anywhere and pinch to resize before you capture. Burned exactly as arranged.

🧭

Standards EXIF

GPS lat/lon/alt/timestamp written into the JPEG, so Photos & Lightroom map it automatically.

🌗

Day & night

Light and dark themes with a one-tap toggle. Follows your system setting by default.

🌡️

Live temperature

The current temperature at your spot (via Open-Meteo) sits on the stamp beside a country flag and the app logo.
