Features

Everything a GPS camera should do

Precise, readable, shareable — and pleasant to use, in day or night.

📸

Geotagged capture

A clean map-on-the-left card burns the locality, full address, Lat / Long, altitude, timestamp and live temperature onto the photo.

🗺️

Live mini-map

A real OpenStreetMap thumbnail of your exact spot — shown live and stamped onto the shot. Tiles are cached & retried so it always embeds.

👆

Open in Maps

Tap the map to jump straight to the coordinates in Google Maps — the link is embedded in the photo's EXIF too.

🌡️

Live temperature

The current temperature at your location via the keyless Open-Meteo API, shown beside a country flag and the app logo.

🎥

Photo & video

Switch to video and record clips to your album. Pinch the preview to zoom smoothly in and out.

🖼️

Full-screen gallery

Tap any shot to open it, swipe between photos, pinch-zoom, then open in Maps or share.

🧲

Move & resize

Drag the info block anywhere and pinch to resize before you capture. It's burned exactly as arranged.

🔁

Portrait & landscape

Rotates smoothly and reflows the controls to a side rail in landscape — the stamp stays upright.

🧭

Standards EXIF

GPS lat/lon/alt/timestamp written into the JPEG, so Google Photos & Lightroom map it automatically.

🌗

Day & night

Light and dark themes with a one-tap toggle. Follows your system setting by default — just like this page.

🛡️

Resilient location

Merges Google Play Services fused location with the platform GPS/network providers, so it works without Play Services.

⚡

Fast & native

Kotlin + Jetpack Compose + CameraX. No heavyweight frameworks — just a quick, responsive shutter.

The stamp

One clean card, all the facts

Map on the left, everything you need on the right — in portrait and landscape.

  • 1
    Live map thumbnail

    A real OpenStreetMap tile of your exact spot, with a pin and a Maps tag — pulled reliably even on flaky networks.

  • 2
    Locality & full address

    A bold locality line above the complete reverse-geocoded address, wrapped to fit.

  • 3
    Precise coordinates

    Lat 12.978361 , Long 77.599380 — six decimals, always readable.

  • 4
    Date, time & note

    MM/DD/YY hh:mm AM/PM plus a Note : Capture by GPS Camera line.

  • 5
    Logo · flag · temperature

    Small chips in the corner: the app logo, your country flag, and the live temperature.
